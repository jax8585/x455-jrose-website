FAQs

How do you decide on a rating for each restaurant?

+

See our Rating Guide for a detailed breakdown of our criteria for rating restaurants. Our team carefully curates the list of restaurants, cafes, and eateries to ensure they meet a high standard of quality and appeal. We evaluate each location based on several factors, including the quality and presentation of the food, the level of service provided, the ambiance and atmosphere of the establishment, and the overall dining experience. Additionally, we actively seek out user feedback and recommendations from our community to keep our listings diverse, fresh, and relevant to our audience's tastes. Whether it's a hidden gem or a popular spot, we aim to feature places that offer something special and memorable for every type of diner.

How do you choose the restaurants and cafes featured on the site?

+

We take a comprehensive approach when selecting the best dining spots to feature on our website. Our team assesses a variety of key elements, such as the quality and flavor of the food, the attentiveness and friendliness of the service, the atmosphere and decor of the space, and the overall dining experience. We also look at the consistency of these factors over time, ensuring that each restaurant delivers a great experience to every guest. To keep our content up to date and diverse, we also take into account feedback from our users, constantly refreshing our listings to reflect new discoveries and popular trends. This ensures our reviews remain relevant and provide our readers with a true reflection of the local dining scene.

Can I leave my own reviews or ratings?

+

At this time, we do not have a platform for user-submitted reviews directly on our website. However, we highly value the experiences and opinions of our readers! We encourage you to share your thoughts with us via our social media channels, where we actively engage with our community. Your feedback might even be highlighted in future updates or special features. We're always looking for fresh perspectives and love hearing about the spots you enjoy, so don't hesitate to reach out!

Are all of your reviews based on personal visits?

+

Yes, all of our reviews are based on first-hand visits from our team. We believe in offering honest and reliable insights, ensuring that every review accurately reflects the experience you can expect when visiting these locations. Our team personally dines at each restaurant, cafe, and eatery we feature, so we can provide you with authentic feedback on food quality, service, ambiance, and overall experience. We aim to give you a clear picture, so you can make well-informed decisions about where to eat.
